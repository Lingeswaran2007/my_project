Simple Naan Recipe

Naan (/nɑːn/) is a leavened, oven-baked or tawa-fried flatbread, that can also be baked in a tandoor. It is characterized by a light and fluffy texture and golden-brown


Prepration Time

  • Total : Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation : 5 minutes

  • Cooking : 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs

  • salt, to taste

  • papper, to taste

  • 1 tablrspoon of butter or oil

Instruction

  1. Beat the eggs : In a bowl beat two eggs with a pinch of salt and papper

  2. Heat the pan : Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat

  3. the naan : Once the butter is melted and bubbing pour in the eggs

  4. Fold and serve : As the naan continues to cook carefully

  5. Enjoy : Serve hot, with additional salt and papper if needed
